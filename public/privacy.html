PRIVACY POLICY

Overview

Evenly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Evenly mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you register.
  • Expense data: Amounts, descriptions, and splits that you enter.
  • Group & friend data: Names and emails of people you add to groups.
  • Usage data: Anonymous in-app event logs to improve the product (no third-party analytics).

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide expense splitting and settlement features.
  • To sync your data across your devices in real time.
  • To send app notifications (e.g. friend requests, settlements).
  • To improve app stability and performance.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on servers with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS). We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Offline Mode

Evenly works offline. Data entered while offline is stored locally on your device and synced to the cloud when connectivity is restored.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Expense data is only visible to the group members you explicitly add.

Data Retention & Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app's Profile settings. Upon deletion, all your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Evenly is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via the app or email.
